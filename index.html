About Us

PT. MANDALASELA EXPRES adalah perusahaan yang bergerak dalam bidang jasa pengiriman ke seluruh wilayah di Indonesia yang berdiri pada tahun 2018.
PT. MANDALASELA EXPRES hadir untuk melayani pengiriman maupun pendistribusian barang khususnya Indonesia Timur dengan akomoda laut baik dengan kapal cepat pelni, kapal Cargo Container, Lct.

PT. MANDALASELA EXPRES is company engaged in the delivery of goods to all regions in Indonesia which was established in 2018.
PT. MANDALASELA EXPRES is now here to serve the delivery and distribution of goods, especially to eastern Indonesia with sea transportation either by fast cargo ship pelni, cargo ship, or roro ship.

Visi

Perusahaan melayani jasa pengiriman dan pendistribusian barang keseluruh wilayah Indonesia dengan CEPAT, AMAN, TEPAT, dan TERPERCAYA.


The company provide serve delivery and distribution of goods throughout Indonesia with FAST, SAVE, APPROPRIATE, and TRUSHTED.

Misi

Memberikan layanan eksekutif dengan tarif ekonomis aman dan terjaga kualitas barang serta dengan SDM terampil dan berkompeten dibidang pengiriman barang.


Providing executive services with economical rates that are save and maintain the quality of goods and with skilled and competent human resources in the field of shipping goods.
